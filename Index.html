An Intelligently British Primary School

Oxbridge School aims to be the top UK Curriculum Primary School in Hong Kong

Welcome from our Principal

I am delighted to welcome you to the Oxbridge School website and here you will be able to find out about our vision of an intelligently British education for your child. We open our doors in August 2021 and would be delighted to welcome your son or daughter to join our initial entry of students with primary classes opening in each of Years 1 to Year 5 taught by experienced and highly qualified teachers. Students with English as a second language will be fully supported in each class.

In addition, we are fully committed to supporting students with Gifted and Special needs. Oxbridge School will offer a high quality, intelligently British, education, built around the framework of the internationally renowned National Curriculum of England and Wales, and in deliberately small class sizes that will never exceed fifteen students – meaning than we can significantly personalise individual learning and markedly enhance your child’s academic progress.

John Shooter,
Principal

Discover Oxbridge School

Oxbridge School opens its doors in August 2021 and aims to be the best provider of the British Primary Curriculum in Hong Kong

At Oxbridge School your child will discover a wealth of important experiences, build lifelong relationships, experience our modern facilities, benefit from our deliberately small class sizes, study in our attractive campus and explore the world of fact and imagination in our new library learning hub. We invite you to visit our school to experience our learning environment for yourself and initiate the process to give your child the best start in life. 

PERSONALISED
We personalise learning in our small class sizes, and aim to nurture students so that they leave us having achieved their personal best.
INOVATIVE

Why Oxbridge School?

Oxbridge School will provide a broad, ambitious and challenging education for children from Year 1 to Year 6 where class sizes will never exceed 15 students and each child will be individually known by the whole school community.

Based upon the internationally renowned National Curriculum of England and Wales and taught in the nurturing and personalised environment of our deliberately small and selective school.
